      • User Experience

        Our UX designers primarily concern is to define how the product feels. A given design problem has no single right answer. Our UX designers explore many different approaches to solving a specific user problem. The broad responsibility of our UX designer is to ensure that the product logically flows from one step to the next.

      • UI & Branding

        Our UX designers and Brand specialists will focus on how the product is laid out and if the message of the brand is passing throughout your digital presence. They are in charge of designing each screen or page with which a user interacts and ensuring that the UI visually communicates the path that a UX designer has laid out and the message that your Brand wants to express.

      • Industrial

        Our team of industrial designers develops the concepts for manufactured products. We have expertise in small appliances, white goods, electronics and toys. We combine art, business, and engineering to make products that people use every day. Contains a high level of realism to fully define product appearance as a perspective view and this is what is in common in all our Industrial designers.

      • Cloud

        When we refer to Cloud architecture we mostly refer to the Back end, the cloud section of the system. Our Architects can take care of the Servers, databases and middleware. PHP, Python and Ruby are among the languages that our developers can master, but are not limited to. We build the right architecture for your needs.

      • Systems

        As Altrove specializes in the combination of hardware and software, the system architecture is a key capability of our structure. Our systems architects interface with multiple stakeholders in order to understand the various levels of requirements, the domain, the viable technologies, and anticipated development. Our team determines multiple design alternatives based on all identified constraints (cost, schedule, space, power, safety, usability, reliability, maintainability, availability, and so on), selecting the most suitable options for you.

      • Software

        Our software Engineers are able to the design, develop, maintain, test, and evaluate the performance of any product developed by us for you. We can develop any type of app, all with the required certifications and with different level of security.

      • Hardware

        Electro-Mechanical engineering, Food Engineering and Chemical Engineering are at the core of Altrove Innovation since its creation in 2015.Our team of engineer had been involved in the development of very well-known consumer products as Nespresso®, Laurastar®, Nescafé DolceGusto® and much more. The main target for our team of engineer is to do not create gadgets but intelligent, connected product that fits the IoT revolution in and out of home.

      • Firmware

        Firmware engineers are the key component of Altrove Innovation business model. Our senior team, due to a long experience in embedded electronic can integrate software and hardware effortlessly and create the product you had in mind. Our firmware engineers are responsible for the development and implementation of software for industry specific applications and systems. They plan, improve and manage software applications from inception to use.

      • Rapid Prototyping

        In this phase Altrove uses prototyping, rapid prototyping/cnc, sculpture, urethane casting, short run manufacturing, food processing equipment and a wide reange of digital technologies to develop functional prototypes, let them be hardware, software, house appliances (from coffee machines to washing machines), consumer products (comprehensive of food & beverages), or medical devices. We build physical and/or virtual prototypes depending on the scope and convenience.

      • Pilot Launch

        A Pilot is intended to be production quality - albeit on a smaller scale. To reach the pilot phase it is necessary to validate the prototype function and characteristics for production, to minimize risk, to define costs in the product Bill Of Materials (BOM) and production processes, and defined the life cycle of the product. Throughout our knowledge in electro-mechanical engineering, Finite Element Analysis (FEA), food chemistry, digital technologies, product development and so on, we can fulfill all needs of this phase. Altrove and its network of experts have more than a decade of experience in developing pilots for home appliances, food & beverages and digital solutions.

      • Mass Production

        We have production capabilities for small and large appliances, food products and have access to chemical labs, virtual spaces and web-based data storage. In collaboration with partners we can produce injection molds, metal folded sheets, polymeric and metallic detailed products throughout different 3d printing technologies.
