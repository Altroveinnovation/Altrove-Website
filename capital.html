Why Altrove?

Utilizing the power of Altrove's deep network, we match disruptive startups with motivated investors & corporate partners.

    • Technology Acceleration

      Deep connections with high-impact laboratories and programs at top-tier academic institutions, and close ties with leading inventors and technologists in a wide range of industries around the globe.

    • Venture Capital

      We support groundbreaking technologies in making an impact on the world. Our network of investors is ever-expanding and every single one is aligned with our mission, focused on technologies and problems they care about.

    • Strategic Partnerships

      Our partners are driving structural changes in the field of corporate venture. 90 days time-to-market for most innovation projects & initiatives in Europe, Americas, Asia, Africa & Middle East.

Altrove is designed to address the unique needs of early-stage startups at the intersection of hardware and software. Most institutional investors have moved upstream and back hardware companies that have completed product development. Altrove Capital fills the gap in early stage hardware financing, investing in pre-seed, pre-product companies. We believe capital is a commodity and people are not. We believe people are at the center of great products and great products are at the center of exceptional companies. We love lean, technical teams and thrive on helping founders bridge the gap between idea and product/market fit. An investment from Altrove Capital brings a full stack team devoted to helping you get to market faster, raise smart follow-on capital, and build a foundation for success.

  • Stage

    Several stages. Technology development up to $250k value.

  • Sector

    Intersection of hardware and software. Focused on companies with high customer lifetime value, usually with recurring revenue.

  • Platform

    Expertise and infrastructure. Partners who have built multi-billion dollar companies. A full-time engineering team that has shipped millions of units.
